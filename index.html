About Nusarat

Nusarat means “victory” in Arabic. We believe every person deserves to find their victory over struggle, pain, and silence.

💜 Our Mission

Nusarat provides free, confidential mental health and emotional support to individuals in the Asian community who may be experiencing abuse, cultural pressures, family conflict, or emotional distress. We understand the unique challenges faced by South Asian women, men, and young people.

🤝 Our Approach

We offer a culturally sensitive, non-judgmental space. Our trained support workers speak your language and understand your background. Whether you need someone to talk to, guidance through a crisis, or a confidential referral, we are here for you.

🔒 Confidentiality

Everything you share with us is 100% confidential. We will never contact your family, share your details, or take any action without your consent. Your safety and privacy are our top priority.

🌏 Community Focused

Based in Yorkshire and serving communities across the North of England, Nusarat works closely with local organisations, mosques, community centres, and healthcare providers to ensure support is accessible to those who need it most.

Our Services

We provide a range of free, confidential support services tailored to your needs.

💬

Emotional Support

One-to-one confidential listening sessions with a trained support worker who understands your culture and language.

🚶

Abuse Support

Help for those experiencing mental, physical, or psychological abuse. We can guide you through your options safely.

👪

Family & Marriage

Support with family conflict, in-law issues, marriage difficulties, and relationship pressures within the community.

🏠

Immigration Support

Guidance for those facing partner-related immigration issues, including spousal visa concerns and dependency situations.

💖

Wellbeing & Self-Image

Support for stress, body image issues, self-esteem, and cultural or religious identity confusion.

📞

Crisis Helpline

If you are in immediate danger, we can help connect you with emergency services and provide urgent guidance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this service really free?

Yes. Nusarat is a completely free service. You will never be charged for any support we provide.

Will my family find out?

No. Everything is 100% confidential. We will never contact your family or share your information with anyone without your explicit permission.

What languages do you support?

Our team can support you in English, Urdu, Punjabi, Bengali, Arabic, and other languages. Just let us know your preference.

How do I get support?

Simply fill in the referral form below. You can share as much or as little as you feel comfortable with. A member of our team will call you back at a time that suits you.

Can I refer someone else?

Yes. If you know someone who needs support, you can fill in the referral form on their behalf with their consent. Just let us know in the form.

What is the Quick Exit button?

The red “Quick Exit” button in the bottom corner will instantly take you to a safe website (Google) if you need to leave this page quickly for your safety.
